Method Comparison Demo — n=250,000
Worked example comparing all 7 methods across three pipeline tiers: Naive RINT, Wang et al. 2019 (no-RINT), and PolyVAR. Shows false positive inflation in Block 1 mQTL SNPs and clean detection of the true vQTL by PolyVAR.
Power & BH-FDR Tables — n=250,000
Analytical simulation across a 10×10 (β, δ) grid. Shows FPR inflation at β≥0.28 for Levene/BF/Bartlett and zero contamination for PolyVAR.
